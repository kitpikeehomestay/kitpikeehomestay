ABOUT US

Welcome To KitpiKee Homestay

Welcome to Kitpi Kee Homestay, where comfort meets nature in perfect harmony. We invite you to experience the warmth of our home, nestled in the serene beauty of Tawang. With spacious rooms thoughtfully designed for relaxation and equipped with modern amenities, we offer a peaceful retreat from the everyday rush.

Our homestay is more than just a place to stay — it's a place where guests are treated like family. Whether you're seeking quiet moments of reflection or an escape into nature, Kitpi Kee Homestay promises a memorable and rejuvenating experience.

Free Parking

Enjoy free parking for up to 5 vehicles, ensuring convenience for all our guests.

Serene Valley View

Our homestay offers breathtaking valley views and majestic snow-covered mountains, creating a serene winter escape.

Farm to Table

At Kitpi Kee Homestay, we embrace farm-to-table dining, growing our own vegetables locally because we believe in knowing where our food comes from.

Homegrown Liquor

Experience our homegrown liquor, crafted from local fruits and occasionally infused with medicinal plants, available for adventurous tourists.

TV HD

Enjoy TV facilities in our rooms for entertainment and relaxation during your stay at Kitpi Kee Homestay.

Explore The Most Beautiful Places Around Our Homestay

Nestled in Tawang district, Kitpi Village offers a captivating blend of natural beauty and Monpa culture. Experience traditional customs in Shernup Village, savor local cuisine in Jamkhar, and relax in sacred hot springs.

Hike to the Chag-zom Bridge, then unwind at Kitpi Kee Homestay, where local hospitality creates lasting memories in this enchanting destination.
